Teacher Tools

Apps to simplify classroom admin and student tracking.

StudentTracker

A teacher-facing app for recording observations, lunchtime reflections, blue slips, King’s Credits and RTC referrals. Supports PDF/email export and date-based filtering so behaviour records can be recalled by student, class, week, month, or year.

  • Platform: iOS (SwiftUI)
  • Status: In testing (TestFlight)
  • Use: Daily behaviour and wellbeing logging

TeacherDiary

Weekly planner and diary app for teachers. Imports Compass timetables (CSV), stores lesson notes, and allows quick capture of class highlights, issues and follow-ups.

  • Platform: iOS (SwiftUI)
  • Status: Prototype / internal testing
  • Use: Planning, reflection and evidence collection

Christian Studies & Bible Apps

Interactive tools to help students engage with Scripture.

ChristianStudiesAI

A lesson companion for Years 7–12 Christian Studies. Each lesson combines an opening prayer, key verse, in-class activity, AI-driven Q&A, and a simple colouring or game element to keep students engaged during 41-minute periods.

  • Platform: iOS (SwiftUI + OpenAI API)
  • Use: Guided AI conversations around Bible passages

FaithBuilder / Bible Study App

Personal Bible study helper with topic-based studies stored in Firestore and search powered by a KJV Bible API. Users can save studies locally for offline access and review them later.

  • Platform: iOS (SwiftUI + Firebase)
  • Use: Structured Bible study with saved notes

Music & Worship Tools

Apps to support worship teams and personal practice.

GuitarSongBook

A worship songbook app that stores lyrics and chords, supports key transposition, playlists, and fast song lookup. Ideal for worship leaders and musicians who want a digital folder instead of loose sheets.

  • Platform: iOS (SwiftUI)
  • Features: Transpose, search, playlists, JSON song import

Pentecostal Songbook

A categorized collection of general and islander worship songs based on Liberty Fellowship resources. Designed to make it easy to browse songs by theme and language.

  • Platform: iOS (SwiftUI)
  • Features: Categories, fast lookup, lyrics display
